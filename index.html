Japanese bonsai museum

There was a museum named Shunkaen Bonsai that was owned and operated by bonsai master, Kunio Kobayashi. It had many old bonsai trees and was located in the city of Tokyo. There are many old bonsai trees present in the world. In order of age, the youngest tree in the sequence is a chabo-hiba cypresse, then a Yamaki pine, a Sandai Shogun No Matsu, a red pine bonsai, a bonsai tree at the Shunkaen nursery, an old juniper bonsai tree, and the last one is a Ficus bonsai tree.
